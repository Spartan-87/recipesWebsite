Spaghetti Carbonara

Home

Description

This classic Italian dish is creamy, savory, and incredibly satisfying.

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 150g guanciale
  • 2 large eggs
  • 100g Pecorino Romano cheese
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente.
  2. In a pan, cook guanciale until crispy.
  3. Beat eggs and mix with grated cheese and pepper.
  4. Drain pasta and combine with guanciale and egg mixture.
  5. Serve immediately with extra cheese and pepper.
